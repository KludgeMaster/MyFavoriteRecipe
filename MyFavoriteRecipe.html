How to Pan Fry the Perfect Steak

Save your BBQ grill for a beach party!

Enjoy perfect steaks today!!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
HIDE ALL PICTURES
  1. Pick your steak

    2. Go to your favorite place for a great meat.

  2. Prepare your meat

    3. Make sure your meat is not frozen.

    Warm up your steak to about room temperature.

    Season it with salt and pepper.

  3. Cook the steak

    4. Make sure to seer every side of the steak so that the 'juice' of the meat stays while being cooked.

    Flip the steak every minute, and cook it for the total of 8 minutes.

  4. Wait for 5 minutes

    5. After putting the steak on the plate, wait for 5 minutes before serving.

    This will make the steak more flavor.
